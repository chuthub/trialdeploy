By A Lake, Today

        I sat by a lake today.
        Hours passed
        as I sat there,
        until in the distance came a sound.
        A rustle at first,
        then absolute pageantry.
        I couldn't make it out at first. 
        It lilted along the grassy shore
        dodging in and out of trees,
        dipping into the chilly waters,
        dancing with the wind.
        It passed as it came,
        and i sat there alone,
        on the shore of that glassy water.
